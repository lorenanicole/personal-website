Lorena Mesa

Political analyst turned professional coder Lorena is the type of person known to ask many questions, both big and small. Relentlessly curious and always hungry to learn Lorena spends her free time teaching (and gaming!) in the community at large. Girl Develop It instructor, Dev Bootcamp guide, and Adler Planetarium STEM mentor Lorena is a fervent believer in diversifying tech by creating accessible beginner friendly educational opportunities for women and other underrepresented groups. Most days you can find Lorena coding with Sprout Social's Platform Engineering team.
